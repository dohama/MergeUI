Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Introduction

MergeUi is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our dashboard template subscription service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect your email address, display name, and profile information when you register. If you sign in via GitHub OAuth, we receive your GitHub user ID, email address, display name, and profile picture from GitHub. If you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your Google user ID, email address, display name, and profile picture from Google. Payment information (card details, billing address) is processed and stored exclusively by Lemonsqueezy; we never see or store your full card number. We also collect usage data such as pages visited, features used, and download history.

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to provide and maintain the service, process subscriptions and deliver templates, communicate important updates and product announcements, and improve the user experience through aggregated analytics.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS 1.3, hashed passwords, and regular security audits. Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Lemonsqueezy for payment processing and subscription management, Supabase for database hosting and authentication, and Google Analytics for aggregated usage analytics. Each provider operates under their own privacy policy and data processing agreements.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management, and analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand how our service is used. For EU visitors, we display a cookie consent banner; you may choose to accept all cookies or only essential ones. You may also disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect service functionality.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request data portability, restrict processing, or object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mergeui.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently erased within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

9. Children's Privacy

MergeUi is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a prominent notice on our website at least 30 days before they take effect. Your continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at privacy@mergeui.com.
